The focus is on how the MERN stack helps in building scalable and dynamic educational platforms while implementing AI-driven functionalities like chatbots, recommendation systems, and automated assessments.

The paper also explores how the use of distributed AI technologies can optimize educational platforms by reducing response times, improving scalability, and enhancing content personalization. This research intends to bridge the gap between conventional e-learning platforms and modern AI-powered educational systems.
